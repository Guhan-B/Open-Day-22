Message from the Head of Department

Department of Computer Technology is established with state-of-the-art infrastructure facilities and a professional teaching fraternity for imparting quality education towards developing globally renowned students. The curriculum supporting Outcome Based Education framed to meet industrial needs helps students develop professional skills to bridge the academia-industry gap. Students are regularly monitored and mentored by an experienced team of faculty advisors. The department has established research laboratories in advanced domains of computing for promoting research activities. Students are motivated to work on industry/academia research problems and are guided by the faculty members to publish the work in reputed journals and conferences.

- Dr. P. Jayashree
